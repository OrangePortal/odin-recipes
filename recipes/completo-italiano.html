Completo Italiano

Description

Hot dogs are… not really a thing in Chile. If you gave any Chilean a bun with a sausage, a sauce or two and nothing else, they’d be confused, and a little bit dissapointed.

Not to throw any hate towards regular hot dogs, they taste great, but we Chileans are deeply in love with our Italianos.

Completo means complete or full. Italiano means Italian. You probably get the 'full' part, but, why Italian? Isn’t this a Chilean recipe?

Well, let’s take a look at the ingredients. First we have the usual bread and sausage, and then we have tomatoes, avocado and mayonnaise. Red, green, and white, the colors of the Italian flag!

So, let’s see how we make them.
